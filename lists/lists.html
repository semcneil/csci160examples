How to make cooked playdough

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup salt
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar (optional, for improved elasticity)
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • Food coloring

Directions

  1. In a saucepan, combine the flour, salt, and cream of tartar.
  2. Add the oil and food coloring to the boiling water and stir.
  3. Slowly add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients in the saucepan.
  4. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture pulls away from the sides and forms a ball.
  5. Transfer the dough to a counter and knead until it is silky-smooth.
